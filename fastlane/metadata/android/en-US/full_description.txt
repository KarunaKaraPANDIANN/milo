  Progressive Overload app to help you increase your workout intensity gradually over days. The app supports the following preferences:
  Set starting weights, reps, or durations for each exercise.
  Customize daily increments for weight, reps, or workout duration.
  Track your progress with detailed logs and charts.
  Set workout frequency and rest days to match your training plan.
  Other features:
  Visual progress tracking to keep motivation high.
  Simple and clean interface for easy daily use.
  Export your workout data for backup or sharing.
  Three main workout modes:
  Strength training (weight-based increments)
  Endurance training (reps or time-based increments)
  Custom workouts tailored to your goals
