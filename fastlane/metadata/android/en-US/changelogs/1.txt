Initial release of Milo - Progressive Overload Workout Tracker

Features:
- Set starting weights, reps, or durations for each exercise
- Customize daily increments for weight, reps, or workout duration
- Track your progress with detailed logs and charts
- Set workout frequency and rest days
- Visual progress tracking
- Simple and clean interface
- Export workout data for backup or sharing
- Three workout modes: Strength, Endurance, and Custom
